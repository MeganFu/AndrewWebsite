morningside campus
morningside heights, new york / 2012

Located on the periphery of Morningside Park in New York, the new art school complex forms a gateway to the Columbia University campus from Harlem. Program is sunken into the ground in order to minimize intrusion into the park. A series of courtyards help to organize and light adjacent classrooms and gallery spaces.
