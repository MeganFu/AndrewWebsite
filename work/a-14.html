vox populi
glasgow, scotland / 2010

Open Competition – 3rd place

Vox Populi is a proposal for a temporary kiosk at the James Morrison Street Festival. The competition brief required that the kiosk be built out of 40 1675 x 1485mm cardboard sheets without glue. We adopted a triangular module to improve stability and then stacked 3 different sizes of wedges in rows to generate variation in the façade. A small ledge inside the pavilion provides a resting spot for visitor information.
