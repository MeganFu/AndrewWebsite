soft space
1407 broadway, new york / 2014

1407 Broadway Competition

For over a century office typologies have evolved to reflect vastly different organizational methods, hierarchies, and cultures, yet at their core they are defined by attitudes and conceptions about privacy. We propose a revised partition that provides a softer space that is both malleable and flexible. The future office is not a tabula rasa condition, but a smart office that is more accommodating to the varying agendas, needs, and cultures of individuals.

Collaboration w/ Michael Jefferson and Suzanne Lettieri
