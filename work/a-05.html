cast
crevelcore, bologna / 2013

Post Quake Visions Competition - Finalist

Italy is a prisoner of its past. Then, a disaster. A quake, a shake, the break is made: there is opportunity for growth out of loss. What Crevalcore and depopulating Italian cities do not need is another icon and more tourists, but a restabilization of its local core. We propose a cast on Crevalcore’s wound, to facilitate Crevalcore’s reconstruction and rebirth as Crevalcore: never the Venice or the Rome, but home to over 13,000 stories… simple, spontaneous, and special.
