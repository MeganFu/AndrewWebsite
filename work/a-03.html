alley orangerie

montreal, quebec
2013

Alley Orangerie appropriates an empty lot to create a refuge for plants and people during the winter. The traditional orangerie is supplemented with additional amenities to transform the once aristocratic and agricultural typology into a cozy neighborhood living room where people can gather and relax along with their plants.
