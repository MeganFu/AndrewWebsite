market / lab
paris, france / 2011

ArchMedium Competition

Culinary institution by day, open restaurant by night, the Paris Market Lab is a dynamic restaurant that will transform throughout the course of the day. The site is split into two main components, “market” and “lab”. During the day students learn from master chefs in the lab and kitchens. As night sets, massive top-hung panels swing open in spectacular fashion, changing from walls to floors. The closed private institution transforms into an open public restaurant, revealing its culinary treats and wonders.
