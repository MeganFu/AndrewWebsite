slope
socrates sculpture park, queens / 2014

Folly Competition

Slope is the product of our interest in the increasingly scarce amount of public space that is constantly encroached by private interests. The proposal takes shape in the form of a shingled roof that becomes a public gathering space for visitors. Come to the SLOPE to eat lunch, sunbathe, or play a game of chess; a series of domestic programs enacted on a public stage. Think I.M.B.Y instead of N.I.M.B.Y. A roof over our heads. White picket fences. Backyards littered with toys and other signs of domestic bliss. Ah! The American Dream.
